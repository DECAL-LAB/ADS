Gen AI Infrastructure for Enhancing App Response

Modern environment-aware apps can increase utility and effectiveness by providing conversational interaction with knowledge and data available to the app. E.g., GPS location interaction with maps and points of interest can be made human speech minable through the phone hardware.

This project aims to develop a general infrastructure for LLM integration into environment-aware applications. In particular, we will focus on an emergency planning app instance, for various trips. Users of that app will able to get personalized safety and emergency-planning, covering everything from back-country adventures—to everyday trips around town.
